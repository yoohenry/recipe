How to make spicy chicken soup

This is how you make a spicy chicken soup like a pro

But, first

Here's the list of ingredients you need for this recipe

  1. Main ingredients
    • carrots
    • onions
    • potatotes

  2. Sauce
    • soy sauce
    • sugar
    • chili powder
    • garlics

  3. Miscellaneous ingredients
    • red/green chilli peppers
    • green onions

5 steps to making spicy chicken soup

  1. Cut the main ingredients into portions smaller than chicken
  2. Add sugar into water and boil the chicken
  3. Once the water boils, add main ingredients in the order of potatoes, carrots, and onions couple minutes apart from each other
  4. Add rest of the ingredients and the sauce

That's it!

You can now enjoy your dish!
